## Stack 

| Data Structure | Avg Access | Avg Search | Avg Insert | Avg Delete | Space (Worst) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Stack** | $O(n)$ | $O(n)$ | $O(1)$ | $O(1)$  | $O(n)$ |

MUST REMEMBER, Stack is LIFO (Last In, First Out).

Nothing too special about stack, just remind some of its application:
* String & Brackets check
* Depth-First Search and Backtracking
* Monotonic Stack: A miracle that can help an algorithm from $O(n^2)$ to $O(n)$
